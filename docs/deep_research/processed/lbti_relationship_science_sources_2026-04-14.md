# LBTI Relationship Science Sources

Date: 2026-04-14

Purpose:
Record the relationship-science frameworks currently mapped into `LBTI` dimensions, question blocks, and on-site methodology copy.

## Source Registry

1. Adult attachment review
Title: Attachment in Adulthood: Recent Developments, Emerging Debates, and Future Directions
Publisher: Annual Review of Psychology
URL: https://www.annualreviews.org/doi/10.1146/annurev-psych-010418-102813
Used for:
- `回应敏感`
- `自主边界`
- `撤离防御`
Why it matters:
- adult attachment explains why some people become highly response-sensitive, some distance, and some oscillate between approach and withdrawal

2. Perceived partner responsiveness
Title: Perceived Partner Responsiveness and Wellbeing
Publisher: Oxford University Press
URL: https://academic.oup.com/book/46095/chapter/404618236/chapter-pdf/58126888/oso-9780197567579-chapter-6.pdf
Used for:
- `回应敏感`
- `表达与脆弱`
- `自主边界`
Why it matters:
- feeling understood, cared for, and respected is a core mechanism behind relationship wellbeing and subjective security

3. Bids for connection
Title: 3 Ways to Make a Better Bid for Connection
Publisher: The Gottman Institute
URL: https://www.gottman.com/blog/3-ways-to-make-a-better-bid-for-connection/
Used for:
- `回应敏感`
- `表达与脆弱`
Why it matters:
- many couple interactions are small bids for attention, affection, reassurance, or engagement

4. Repair attempts
Title: Homework Assignment: Repair Attempts
Publisher: The Gottman Institute
URL: https://www.gottman.com/blog/weekend-homework-assignment-repair-attempts/
Used for:
- `冲突修复`
- `撤离防御`
Why it matters:
- relationship quality depends not only on conflict intensity but also on whether repair attempts happen and get received

5. Commitment / investment model
Title: Commitment and Its Theorized Determinants: A Meta-Analysis of the Investment Model
Publisher: Personal Relationships
URL: https://static1.1.sqspcdn.com/static/f/984219/13434258/1311966710690/Le_Agnew_2003_PR.pdf
Used for:
- `承诺清晰`
Why it matters:
- commitment is shaped by satisfaction, alternatives, and investment, which maps well to needs for definition, exclusivity, and reliability

6. Dyadic coping
Title: The Associations of Dyadic Coping and Relationship Satisfaction Vary between and within Nations: A 35-Nation Study
Publisher: Frontiers in Psychology
URL: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2016.01106/full
Used for:
- `冲突修复`
Why it matters:
- handling stress together is associated with relationship satisfaction across cultures

## LBTI Mapping

### 回应敏感
- theoretical anchors:
  - attachment security
  - perceived partner responsiveness
  - bids for connection
- current question focus:
  - reply timing
  - contact rhythm
  - whether support shows up during low mood

### 表达与脆弱
- theoretical anchors:
  - partner responsiveness
  - bids for connection
- current question focus:
  - direct signaling
  - showing care
  - public/private expression
  - reopening after conflict

### 冲突修复
- theoretical anchors:
  - repair attempts
  - dyadic coping
- current question focus:
  - silence tolerance
  - re-entry after conflict
  - who offers a step down
  - whether couples revisit and process conflict

### 自主边界
- theoretical anchors:
  - attachment dynamics
  - responsiveness/autonomy balance
- current question focus:
  - reporting/check-ins
  - space needs
  - frequency of contact
  - perceived intrusiveness

### 承诺清晰
- theoretical anchors:
  - investment model
- current question focus:
  - exclusivity
  - future planning
  - definition of the relationship
  - consistency and follow-through

### 撤离防御
- theoretical anchors:
  - attachment avoidance / defensive withdrawal
  - failed repair
- current question focus:
  - cooling off versus pulling out
  - chat history / traces after break
  - comeback handling
  - exit style

## Product Note

`LBTI` remains an entertainment product. The scientific layer is used to improve:
- dimension coverage
- question realism
- explanatory credibility
- user sense that results come from recognizable relationship mechanisms, not random archetype naming
