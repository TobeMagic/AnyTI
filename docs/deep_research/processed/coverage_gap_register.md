## Covered

- Public media analysis of why SBTI spread

- User-facing lessons for IA, category theming, archetype language, and screenshot-oriented spread

## Not Covered Yet

### Original source stability
- The original creator-controlled link and video trail are unstable or partially removed.
- Current product observations may rely on mirrors or secondary reporting.

### Platform-specific preview behavior
- Xiaohongshu, WeChat, and Moments share-card behavior has not been manually verified on a live AnyTI build.
- This matters before Phase 3 poster/share execution.

### Quantitative funnel data
- No direct analytics from the original SBTI product are available.
- Media reports cite public indices and discussion volume, but not detailed conversion funnels.

### Cross-category naming performance
- We know the direction should be abstract, humorous, and socially legible.
- We do not yet know which naming pattern works best per category (work, love, study, travel, etc.).

## Recommended Follow-Up

1. During Phase 2, create a naming benchmark sheet for WBTI archetypes.
2. During Phase 3, manually test poster and link-preview behavior on target social platforms.
3. Before cloning to LBTI/SBTI/STBI, compare which label formats get the strongest screenshot/relay reaction.
